Yes! ExploitZ3r0BOT shows a mandatory verification popup on every single page load. Users cannot access your site content or click any buttons until they complete the captcha. The site content is completely locked and blurred until verification is successful.

What triggers a block?

Multiple factors trigger blocking: clicking more than 3 times, clicking too fast (under 800ms), no mouse movement, extended inactivity with page focus, and other suspicious behavioral patterns that indicate automated access.

Will real users get blocked?

Real users who interact normally will never be blocked. The detection thresholds are designed to catch bots while allowing normal human behavior. If someone does get blocked, they simply reload the page and verify normally.

How does the button lockdown work?
